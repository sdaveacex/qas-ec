About Us

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    The QUANTUM AEROSPACE RESEARCH INSTITUTE (QAS) was conceived in 2013 as a Spin-Off of the Ecuadorian Civil Space Agency (EXA), after participating in the launch campaigns of the first constellation of Ecuadorian satellites NEE-01 PEGASO and NEE-02 KRYSAOR.We believe that the future of the human species is intimately linked to the cosmos as a solution to the growing situation of lack of resources at a planetary level. We are an active part of several scientific groups worldwide that intend to consolidate humanity as a multiplanetary species at the level of the solar system.

  • An Agency is Born

    Since our inception, we have oriented our vision and mission to scientific research: basic and applied, leading to significant advances in the aerospace industry. Our activities are aimed at research and development (R&D) processes, which allow us to generate solutions, products, systems and subsystems in the aerospace field.

  • Transition to Full Service

    We firmly believe that the rise of Quantum Science and Technology will mark a turning point in the Information Technology sector, and the Generation of Energy for our civilization, and therefore a large part of our R&D processes are directed towards this sector. We are specialists in the conception, design and implementation of scientific and industrial payloads that need to be exposed to microgravity environment.
